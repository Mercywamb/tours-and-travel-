Activities

Wildlife Tour

Relax and rejuvenate at a luxury safari lodge or tented camp, where you can enjoy spa treatments, massages, and breathtaking views of the surrounding natural beauty.

Coastal Visits

Explore the beautiful coastline and white sandy beaches of Diani or Malindi, where you can relax, swim, and go snorkeling or diving..

trip to massai mara

Take a hot air balloon ride over the Maasai Mara to spot the "Big Five" (lion, leopard, elephant, rhino, buffalo) and other wildlife.

cultural diversity

Indulge in the local cuisine by trying traditional Kenyan dishes such as nyama choma (grilled meat), ugali (maize porridge), and sukuma wiki (collard greens).

wildlife

Take a boat tour on Lake Naivasha or Lake Nakuru to see thousands of flamingos, along with other bird species and wildlife.
